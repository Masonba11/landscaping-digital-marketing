What are Landscaping Ads?

Landscaping ads are paid advertisements that appear when homeowners search for landscaping services on Google, or when they browse Facebook and Instagram. Unlike traditional advertising (billboards, radio, print), you can target exactly who sees your ads, track every dollar spent, and adjust campaigns in real-time based on performance.

We specialize in creating ads specifically for landscaping companies. We know which keywords convert, what messaging resonates with homeowners, and how to structure campaigns to maximize ROI.

Why Landscaping Companies Need Paid Advertising

Here's the challenge: even with great SEO, it takes 3-6 months to see significant results. Meanwhile, your competitors might be running ads and capturing leads you're missing. Paid advertising solves this by:

  • Generating leads immediately: Start getting phone calls and quote requests within days of launching campaigns
  • Filling seasonal gaps: Boost visibility during slow periods when referrals drop off
  • Testing new services: Promote new offerings (like hardscaping or irrigation) to see demand before investing heavily
  • Competing with larger companies: Appear at the top of search results even if competitors outrank you organically
  • Controlling your budget: Set daily or monthly spending limits and pause campaigns anytime

Types of Landscaping Ads We Manage

1. Google Search Ads

These ads appear at the top of Google search results when homeowners search for keywords like "landscaper near me" or "lawn care services." They're highly targeted because they only show to people actively searching for your services.

We optimize ad copy, landing pages, and bidding strategies to maximize conversions while minimizing cost per lead.

2. Google Display Ads

Display ads show on websites your potential customers visit. We create visual ads that showcase your work and retarget visitors who've been to your website but didn't contact you yet.

3. Facebook & Instagram Ads

We create image and video ads that appear in Facebook and Instagram feeds. These ads are perfect for showcasing before-and-after photos, promoting seasonal services, or building brand awareness in your service area.

4. Local Service Ads

Google Local Service Ads appear at the very top of search results with a "Google Guaranteed" badge. They're ideal for service-area businesses and only charge when someone calls or messages you directly.
