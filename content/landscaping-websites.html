Why Your Current Website Might Be Costing You Leads

If your website was built years ago, or if it's a generic template, it's probably hurting your business. Here's what happens with outdated or poorly designed sites:

  • Slow loading times: 53% of visitors abandon sites that take more than 3 seconds to load
  • Poor mobile experience: 60% of searches happen on mobile—if your site doesn't work well on phones, you're losing customers
  • Confusing navigation: Visitors can't find your services or contact information, so they leave
  • No clear call-to-action: Even interested visitors don't know how to get a quote or schedule service
  • Outdated design: A dated website makes your business look unprofessional, even if your work is excellent
